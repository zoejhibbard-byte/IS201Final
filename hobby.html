Rock-climbing

I love rock-climbing — both indoor and outdoor. I climb indoors at MetroRock in Littleton, Massachusetts with my brother, and I travel to climb throughout Washington state (including Index, Leavenworth, and Vantage). Climbing is a sport I’m passionate about: it builds strength, focus, and community, and I enjoy both the social indoor gym environment and adventurous outdoor routes.
